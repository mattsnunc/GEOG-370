Covid deaths in each state from 2020-2022

My map answers the question of what public schools in Florida are within a 20 KM range of a hospital.
I figured this would be an important question to ask because people would want their childrens school to be near a
hospital in case of an emergency. With my map people can view what schools are in safe zones
and which ones aren't.
